How to Adjust Your Bike Gears

Follow these steps to fine-tune your bike gears for a smoother ride:

  1. Check the brake pads for wear and alignment.
  2. Loosen the brake cable using an Allen key.
  3. Adjust the pads to align with the wheel rim.
  4. Tighten the brake cable and test the brakes.
  5. Check if the brake cables are aligned
  6. Make sure brakes are working as needed
