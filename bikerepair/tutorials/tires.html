How to Change Your Bike Tires

Follow these steps to replace or repair your bike tires:

  1. Flip the bike upside down so it rests on the handlebars and seat.
  2. Release the brakes and remove the wheel using a quick-release lever or a wrench.
  3. Use tire levers to remove the tire from the rim.
  4. Inspect the tire for punctures or damage, and replace if necessary.
  5. Install the new tire or tube and inflate it to the recommended pressure.
  6. Reattach the wheel and test the bike for proper alignment.
