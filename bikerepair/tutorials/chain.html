How to Repair Your Chain

Follow these steps to fix a broken chain:

  1. Remove the chain using a chain breaker tool.
  2. Inspect for damaged links and remove them.
  3. Reconnect the chain using a new master link.
  4. Ensure the chain moves smoothly over the gears.
